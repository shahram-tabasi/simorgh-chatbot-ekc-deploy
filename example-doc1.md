STATION SERVICE AC SYSTEM

1. SCOPE

This specification covers the minimum requirement for design , manufacture, factory testing

,transportation and installation of the station service AC system.

AC station service shall mean all low voltage AC system required for the station

service of the substation .

2. STANDARDS

The station service AC system shall be designed ,manufactured , installed and tested in

accordance with the latest applicable section (with amendments) of the following standards.

IEC IEC 38 Standard voltages

IEC Current 44 transformers

IEC International 50 Electrotechnical Vocabulary

IEC Low 157 voltage switchgear and controlgear

IEC Low 158 voltage contactors

IEC Voltage 186 transformers

IEC Low 269 voltage fuses with high breaking capacity for industrial and

voltage contactors

IEC Low 408 voltage air break switches , air break

disconnectors , ....

IEC Degrees 529 of protection by enclosures

IEC Ancillary 947 equipment

Any equipment built in accordance with standards,regulation or codes other than I.E.C.

standards or ther publications, even when equivalent, and any waiver to this specification shall

be submitted to engineer for approval

3. DESIGN

The main AC panel boards shall be three phase of four wire with earth bus, and contain

withdrawable air circuit breakers and the AC distribution panel boards shall be three phase

and contain moulded case circuit breaker to feed the switchyard lighting panel board,

building lighting panel board, AC power supply to the relay and control panels in the

control room and relay building , battery chargers , inverters , and all other equipment

requiring AC power, according to related drawings.

Air circuit breakers shall be provided with motor charged spring-operated mechanisms

, and provided with electrically operated trip and close facilities so as to be suitable for

manual and automatic operation. Also it must be withdrawable, poles 4 and three phases.

4. MANUFACTURING

4.1. AC MAIN & DISTRIBUTION PANELS

Panels shall be rear and front access type with modular steel enclosures and lockable hinged

doors. Arrange elements so that access may be made to them from the rear and front for

easy installation , maintenance and replacement . Operations not used for breakers, shall be

covered by removable steel plates.

Busbars shall be designed for full rated current at maximum ambient temperature specified

without their temperature rise limits.

A separate connection on the panel ground bus shall be provided for connection to

grounding system .

These panels shall be constructed of cold rolled steel, 2.5mm in thickness, welded and

reinforced where necessary. Indoor panels shall have IPdegree 42 protection and outdoor

shall have IP54.the main AC panels shall be withdrawable and have type test.

Circuit breakers , with a handle padlock attachment , shall be provided to enable the

breaker to be padlocked in the open or closed position . Panel main circuit breaker

shall be provided with a trip alarm contact for remote indication. Circuit breakers

shall be provided with trip position indication to clearly indicate the breaker trip

position.

solidly earthed neutral and shall be type satisfactorily tested by an approval short circuit

4.2. WIRING AND TERMINALS

All internal wiring shall be continuous from terminal to terminal with no splicing.

Wiring shall be copper conductor with flame retardant PVC insulation . Minimum conductor

sizes shall be 2.5mm for control and protection .

The sizes of earth wires shall be according to the recommendation of IEC60227

Covers and/or door with electrical apparatus attached to them shall be connected to the

switchgear frame via bonding conductors.

Labels on withdrawable units shall be duplicated on the withdrawable part and fixed part.

All wiring shall have cable lugs and shall be terminate in clamp type terminals such that

terminal that direct contacts between screw , bolt or nut and cable lugs are avoided. For CT

secondary wiring , ring type cable lugs shall be used.

The control terminal blocks shall include 20% spare terminals.

All terminals and bare parts shall be covered by epoxy glass.

The standby diesel generator shall be designed for supply to the essential services only , i.e.

4.4.2. GENERATOR

The generator shall be a 4-pole , 3-phase , brushless , synchronous generator for 380 , Hz 50

, rpm 1500. Insulation shall be class F and cooling mode shall be IC 0.in 1 accordance with

IEC publication 34 . It shall be Y-connected (star) and shall withstand both overload and

direct short circuit at the output terminals . The generator shall have an integral (self) A.C

exciter with rotating rectifier and static voltage regulator.

Each generator shall be of the revolving field type. The engine and generator shall be

arranged for mounting on a common bedplate. The generator shall be directly coupled to the

engine.

The diesel generator shall be connected through a circuit breaker, to be provided under this

contract, and a changeover contactor to the main LVAC panel board, as indicated on the

drawings.

The plant shall be suitable in all respects for local , manual , control and shall be capable of

being automatically started up, put on load in the absence of AC electrical supplies . The

diesel generator shall be started manually and / or automatically and , due to the action of the

changeover system the load be connected. The diesel generator shall be capable of running

up to its correct speed and voltage before accepting the application of the full load on

excitation of the generator.

In the event of a min supply failure, after a time delay (by a time relay with settings 0-60S), a

signal will be received and diesel generator shall start immediately . Within seconds 10 after

receiving of signal , diesel generator shall be capable of restoring the voltage & frequency to

the normal rated values and accepting load upon the closure of generator circuit breaker at

C room temperature.

The automatic shutdown shall be provided with a time relay keeping the diesel generator set

running for at least minutes 10 after starting . If the generator breaker is closed within 10

minutes period of starting then the automatic shutdown shall be over – ridden and the

generator shall be kept running.

If the supply returns after the setting time of the time relay minutes 10, the generator shall

immediately be disconnected and the normal supply be connected. The diesel generator set

shall be kept running unloaded for two minutes before it is finally shut down.

Emergency stop switch shall be located close to the diesel generator switch , this switch shall

immediately shut down the set. It shall be lockable in stop position.

The neutral point of the generator stator winding will be solidly connected to the power

station earthing system.

4.4.5. BUILDING

The diesel generator set shall be located in separate building large enough to house the set,

and all auxiliary equipment and indoor auxiliary tank and the station service A.C main

generator distribution boards. Main tank shall be installed in a convenient and accessible and

safe underground. The suitable underground floor slope drain piping shall be connected to

the drains. The noise level at a distance of m 20 from the building shall not exceed dB 75

4.5. STANDBY POWER SOURCE

Provisions shall be made for supplying power to main AC panels via an external source

where the generator set is not required and a separate incoming CB shall be installed in the

panel for this purpose, the source switching logic to be approved by engineer.

publications.

compliance of the equipment with provisions of this specification.
